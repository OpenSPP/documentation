# Registry

**For:** Users (program staff, operators)

The Registry is the foundation of OpenSPP, providing tools for managing individuals, groups, and registrant data. This section covers all aspects of maintaining and working with your beneficiary registry.

## Topics Covered

- **Register Individuals** - Add and manage individual registrants
- **Register Groups** - Create and manage household groups and other group types
- **Import/Export Data** - Bulk data operations for registry management
- **Custom Fields** - Configure custom fields to capture program-specific data
- **Search and Filter** - Find and segment registrants efficiently
- **Data Quality** - Maintain accurate and up-to-date registry information
- **Deduplication** - Identify and merge duplicate records
