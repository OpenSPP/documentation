# Entitlements

**For:** Users (program staff, operators)

Entitlements represent the benefits that beneficiaries are entitled to receive from programs. This section covers managing different types of entitlements including cash transfers, in-kind benefits, and vouchers.

## Topics Covered

- **Cash Entitlements** - Manage cash transfer entitlements
- **In-kind Entitlements** - Manage non-cash benefits (food, goods, services)
- **Vouchers** - Manage voucher-based entitlements
- **Entitlement Approval** - Review and approve entitlement batches
- **Entitlement Validation** - Verify entitlement calculations
- **Entitlement History** - Track entitlement changes over time
- **Special Cases** - Handle exceptions and adjustments
