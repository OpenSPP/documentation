Step 2: Create a Household#

This tutorial is for users who want to register a new household in OpenSPP.

What you'll do#

Create a new household (group) record in the registry, entering basic information about the family.

Before you start#

  • Complete Step 1: Access the Registry

  • Log in with a user that has administrator permissions

  • Have information about the household to register (name, address, contact details)

  • Allow 3-5 minutes to complete this step

The scenario#

The Garcia family has come to your office. You need to create a household record for them before adding individual family members. As a user with administrator priveledge, you can access Browse All and see the New Individual and New Group buttons needed to create registrant records.

Steps#

1. Click Browse All#

From the Registry Search Portal, click Browse All to open the list of registrants where you can create new groups and individuals.

2. Click New Group#

From the Registry Search Portal (or the Browse All view), click the New Group button.

3. Enter household information#

The group form opens. Enter the required information:

Field

Value

Description

Name

Garcia Family

A name to identify this household

Kind

Household

Type of group

Address

123 Main Street

Physical address

Phone

+1-555-0123

Contact number

4. Save the household#

Click Save to create the household record.

5. Verify creation#

After saving, you'll see the household record. Notice the household has no members yet - you'll add them in the next step.

What you accomplished#

You've learned how to:

  • Create a new household (group) in the registry

  • Enter basic household information

  • Save and verify the household record

Key concepts#

Concept

Description

Group

A collection of individuals, typically a household or family

Kind

The type of group (Household, Organization, etc.)

Members

Individuals who belong to the group

Are you stuck?#

Required field missing? Required fields depend on your system configuration. Contact your administrator if a field you need isn't available.

Can't save the record? Check that all required fields are filled in. Required fields typically have an asterisk (*) next to them.

Group kind options not showing? Your administrator may need to configure group types for your installation.

Next Step#

Now that you've created the household, you're ready to add family members. Continue to Step 3: Add Members.
